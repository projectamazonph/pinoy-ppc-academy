# Audience and Personas

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Defines who the platform is designed for and how their starting points differ.

## Primary audience

Filipinos who need to begin a virtual assistant career or shift from another career path, including office employees, BPO workers, freelancers, general virtual assistants, and Amazon support staff who want a higher-value specialization.


## Core personas

| Persona | Starting point | Primary need |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mika, career starter | No VA or Amazon experience; uses a phone heavily; limited budget | A realistic path, setup guidance, and proof that the work is learnable |
| Jomar, BPO career shifter | Strong English and process discipline; unfamiliar with freelancing and Amazon | Translate existing skills into a portfolio and client-facing confidence |
| Ana, general VA | Handles admin, customer service, or product research | Move from task execution to a specialization clients retain |
| Paolo, Amazon operations VA | Understands listings, inventory, or Seller Central but not advertising | Learn PPC quickly without repeating all entry-level VA material |
| Grace, junior PPC operator | Can execute instructions but struggles with diagnosis and strategy | Develop reasoning, account-level thinking, and client communication |


## Common constraints

- Mobile-first access and intermittent internet.
- Limited access to Seller Central or real advertising accounts.
- Fear of making costly mistakes in a live client account.
- Need for plain English and examples in Philippine pesos.
- Pressure to produce a portfolio without violating client confidentiality.
- Uncertainty about finding legitimate clients and avoiding scams.


## Personalization without AI

Onboarding uses a deterministic readiness assessment. Answers place the student into one of three starting routes: zero experience, career shifter, or Amazon-experienced beginner. Rules are transparent and editable by administrators.


| Signal | Routing behavior |
| --- | --- |
| No remote-work experience | Start with work setup, communication, digital tools, and client safety |
| Professional or BPO experience | Shorten general workplace lessons; emphasize freelancing and portfolio translation |
| Existing VA experience | Skip basic tools after passing a readiness check |
| Amazon operations experience | Accelerate through Amazon foundations and enter PPC concepts earlier |
