# Problem and Opportunity

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Defines the learner problems, market gap, and product opportunity without relying on inflated claims.

## Problems to solve

- Most VA career content is broad and stops at generic admin skills.
- Amazon PPC training often assumes access to real accounts and prior Amazon knowledge.
- Students can finish videos without proving they can make safe decisions.
- Career shifters struggle to show transferable skills in a way clients understand.
- Existing simulators rarely reflect the workflow, relationships, and consequences of campaign operations.
- Course claims often exceed the evidence behind them, reducing trust.


## Opportunity

A focused platform can own the gap between free VA orientation and real Amazon advertising competency. The free path solves uncertainty. The paid path solves skill depth. Simulators solve lack of account access. A proof-of-skill system solves the portfolio problem.


## Jobs to be done

| When the learner says... | The product must help them... |
| --- | --- |
| I do not know where to start | Understand the work, requirements, risks, and next action |
| I am changing careers | Map existing experience to VA and PPC competencies |
| I cannot practice in Seller Central | Use realistic simulations with safe feedback |
| I know the terms but freeze during interviews | Practice explaining decisions with evidence |
| I need a portfolio but have no clients | Create original, clearly labeled simulated work samples |
| I want to know whether I am ready | Pass transparent assessments tied to real operator tasks |


## Ethical positioning

- Do not promise a salary, client, or hiring outcome.
- Describe skills, practice, and support precisely.
- Label all simulated data and portfolio artifacts as simulated.
- Use verified founder experience and real product capabilities as primary trust signals.
