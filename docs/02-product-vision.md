# Product Vision

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

The long-term direction and strategic position of the product.

## Vision statement

Build the most practical Filipino-first pathway from zero VA experience to client-ready Amazon PPC operator.


The product should feel like a training floor, not a video library. Every concept should lead to a decision, every decision should lead to practice, and every practice result should create evidence the student can use when applying for work.


## Three-year direction

| Year | Primary focus | Expected product state |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Year 1 | Validate career starter, curriculum, simulations, and paid conversion | A stable direct-to-student academy with measurable completion and hiring outcomes |
| Year 2 | Expand scenario depth, instructor workflows, and employer verification | A recognized assessment and talent signal for Amazon-focused agencies |
| Year 3 | Add team licensing and employer pathways without becoming a generic marketplace | A focused training and talent pipeline for Filipino Amazon advertising specialists |


## Strategic moat

- Filipino context: local income realities, work setup constraints, cultural communication patterns, and Philippine payment methods.
- Operator realism: decision-based simulations instead of passive video completion.
- Source-backed curriculum: reusable content derived from ProjectAmazonPH operating knowledge and public student materials.
- Proof of skill: graded artifacts, decision logs, scenario scores, and public certificate verification.
- Founder credibility: content designed by a working Amazon PPC lead, not a generic course publisher.


## Vision guardrails

- Do not become a broad freelancing marketplace.
- Do not add unrelated VA specializations before the PPC pathway is proven.
- Do not add AI merely because it is fashionable.
- Do not confuse feature count with student outcomes.
- Do not sacrifice mobile usability for dashboard spectacle.
