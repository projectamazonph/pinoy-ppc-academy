# User Journeys

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Maps the principal public, student, instructor, and administrator flows.

## Visitor to starter learner

1. Visitor lands on a career-focused page and sees who the platform is for.
2. Visitor reviews the path from VA starter to PPC operator.
3. Visitor creates a free account through email, Google, or magic link.
4. Onboarding captures current work status, remote experience, Amazon exposure, device access, and goals.
5. A rules-based assessment assigns the correct starting module.
6. The dashboard shows one primary next action and a clear weekly plan.


## Starter to paid student

1. Student completes starter lessons and readiness assessment.
2. The platform explains the recommended paid tier and the capabilities it unlocks.
3. Student selects a tier and pays through PayMongo.
4. Webhook confirmation activates enrollment exactly once.
5. Student receives a receipt and resumes at the recommended specialization lesson.


## Learning loop

```text
Learn concept -> quick check -> guided practice -> scored scenario -> reflection -> next lesson
```


Students cannot earn certification by clicking through content. Required simulations and assessments must meet the published rubric.


## Graduate journey

1. Student completes required modules and simulator gates.
2. Student builds a portfolio from approved simulated artifacts.
3. Student completes a timed capstone and records decision explanations.
4. Instructor or rubric engine grades the submission using deterministic criteria.
5. A verifiable certificate is issued after all gates pass.
6. Career readiness modules prepare the student for applications, interviews, trial tasks, and client onboarding.


## Administrator journey

- Review new enrollments and failed payments.
- Publish lessons and scenario packs through versioned content workflows.
- Review capstones and portfolio submissions.
- Schedule live classes and manage registrations.
- Issue, revoke, and verify certificates.
- Process refunds and investigate access issues through audit logs.
