# Value Proposition and Business Model

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Defines the offer structure, pricing, access model, and conversion logic.

## Value proposition

Start a VA career with a specialization clients can evaluate. Learn Amazon PPC from zero, practice without a live account, and graduate with proof of what you can do.


## Offer ladder

| Level | Price | Core promise |
| --- | --- | --- |
| VA Career Starter | Free | Understand the VA path, prepare your setup, and choose a credible specialization |
| PPC Foundations | PHP 2,999 | Understand advertising fundamentals and perform guided PPC tasks |
| Accelerated Mastery | PHP 5,999 | Complete the full operator curriculum and all core simulations |
| Ultimate Transformation | PHP 9,999 | Receive live instruction, portfolio review, and priority assessment |


## Launch pricing rules

- All launch prices are one-time payments in Philippine pesos.
- Students retain access to purchased content while the product remains active.
- Future recurring membership may cover live community and new scenario packs, but it is not required for launch.
- Discount codes must have clear limits, validity dates, and audit history.
- Refund policy is displayed before checkout and enforced consistently.


## Conversion design

1. The landing page leads with the free VA Career Starter path.
2. Starter lessons establish trust and identify readiness gaps.
3. A completed starter assessment recommends a paid track using deterministic rules.
4. Locked lessons show exactly what capability the paid tier unlocks.
5. Checkout uses PayMongo hosted payments to support common Philippine payment methods.
6. Post-purchase onboarding immediately assigns the next lesson and first simulator task.


## Success metrics

| Metric | Initial target |
| --- | --- |
| Starter completion | At least 45 percent of activated starter learners |
| Starter to paid conversion | Measure baseline first; improve through cohort analysis |
| Paid course completion | At least 60 percent |
| First simulator completion | Within seven days of paid enrollment |
| Refund rate | Below 5 percent |
| Verified portfolio completion | At least 50 percent of mastery graduates |
