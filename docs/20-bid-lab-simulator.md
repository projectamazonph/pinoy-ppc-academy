# Bid Lab Simulator

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Specifies bid, CPC, conversion, and placement decision practice.

## Capabilities

- Display target history, recent metrics, placement performance, break-even limits, and scenario goal.
- Allow increase, decrease, pause, or hold decisions.
- Require a reason before submission.
- Show the economic consequence of each choice after the attempt.
- Teach that holding is a valid decision when evidence is insufficient.


## Core calculations

```text
ACoS = Ad Spend / Ad Sales
ROAS = Ad Sales / Ad Spend
CVR = Orders / Clicks
CPC = Ad Spend / Clicks
Target CPC = Selling Price x CVR x Target ACoS
```


## Scenario variety

- High ACoS with low conversion.
- High ACoS caused by a weak listing rather than bid alone.
- Strong conversion but limited impressions.
- Top-of-search placement profitable while rest-of-search is weak.
- Insufficient clicks to justify a decision.
- Inventory risk that makes scaling unsafe.
