# Executive Summary

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

A concise statement of what will be built, who it serves, and how success will be measured.

## Product thesis

Pinoy PPC Academy is a career-entry and specialization platform for Filipinos who want to start working as virtual assistants or shift away from low-growth generalist work. It starts before Amazon PPC, teaches the realities of remote work and Amazon operations, then develops students into evidence-backed PPC operators who can explain, execute, and defend their decisions.


The platform is not a generic learning management system. The product is organized around five verbs: Learn, Simulate, Diagnose, Prove, and Get Hired.


## Core outcomes

- A complete beginner can understand what virtual assistants do, prepare a viable work setup, and choose Amazon PPC as a specialization.
- A career shifter can identify transferable skills and close gaps without repeating irrelevant beginner material.
- A student can build campaigns, analyze search terms, manage bids and budgets, and diagnose account problems in realistic simulations.
- A graduate leaves with a verified portfolio, assessment history, certificate, and interview-ready evidence of skill.
- Ryan can operate the business from one admin panel without depending on AI or external course platforms.

## Non-negotiables

- [ ] No AI features or AI-dependent grading.
- [ ] No changes to existing repositories.
- [ ] Mobile-first experience for students learning on phones.
- [ ] Realistic but legally distinct Amazon Ads inspired simulations.
- [ ] No invented testimonials, student counts, or performance claims.
- [ ] Tests are written before implementation for every behavior-bearing feature.
- [ ] Release claims require evidence from automated checks and manual review.
