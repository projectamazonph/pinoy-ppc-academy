**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Defines the learning architecture across the complete career pathway.

## Learning pathway

| Stage | Outcome | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. VA Career Starter | Understands remote work, tools, client safety, and specialization choices | Free |
| 2. Amazon Foundations | Understands the marketplace, listings, fulfillment, metrics, and account roles | Free preview plus paid depth |
| 3. PPC Foundations | Understands advertising objects, metrics, targeting, and campaign types | PPC Foundations |
| 4. Campaign Operator | Can safely build and maintain SP, SB, and SD campaigns | Accelerated Mastery |
| 5. PPC Strategist | Can diagnose performance and make account-level decisions | Accelerated Mastery |
| 6. Client-Ready Specialist | Can communicate, document, audit, plan, and defend strategy | Ultimate or certification path |


## Lesson model

1. Concrete opening problem.
2. Three measurable learning outcomes.
3. Plain-English explanation with every acronym defined.
4. Worked example using realistic numbers.
5. Step-by-step operator workflow.
6. Mistakes, warning signals, and recovery actions.
7. Quick check with answer explanations.
8. Practice task or linked simulator scenario.
9. Evidence required before the lesson is considered mastered.


## Instructional rules

- Teach the diagnostic framework from the beginning: bid, listing, inventory or price, and structure.
- Give TACoS a dedicated lesson and calculator because it connects ads to total business health.
- Teach listing readiness before asking students to optimize campaigns.
- Separate branded defense from generic performance campaigns.
- Use down-only bidding as the default teaching baseline unless the scenario justifies another strategy.
- Require students to explain why before changing bids, budgets, negatives, or structure.


## Mastery rules

- Content completion alone does not equal mastery.
- Each stage has knowledge, execution, diagnosis, and communication gates.
- Students may retry, but attempts and changes remain visible in their learning history.
- Rubrics disclose what is scored before the attempt begins.
