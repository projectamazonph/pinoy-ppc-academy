# Scope and Non-Goals

**Product:** Pinoy PPC Academy  
**Repository:** `pinoy-ppc-academy`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Documentation baseline

## Purpose

Defines the launch boundary so the greenfield build remains coherent.

## Launch scope

- Public landing, pricing, curriculum preview, founder, FAQ, and policy pages.
- Email and password, Google sign-in, and email magic-link authentication.
- Free starter track and four paid specialization stages.
- Lessons, quizzes, assignments, deterministic readiness routing, progress, streaks, badges, and certificates.
- Five simulator families: campaign building, bid decisions, search term triage, budget and placement, and account diagnosis.
- Proof-of-skill portfolio generator with simulated-work disclosures.
- PayMongo checkout, payments, discounts, receipts, refunds, and access control.
- Admin operations for users, content, scenarios, grading, commerce, live classes, analytics, and audit logs.
- Unit, integration, end-to-end, accessibility, security, and performance test gates.


## Explicit non-goals

- No AI mentor, chatbot, automated resume writer, or LLM-generated grading.
- No live connection to Amazon Advertising or Seller Central at launch.
- No job marketplace or promise of employment.
- No white-label or multi-tenant agency product at launch.
- No unrelated VA tracks such as bookkeeping, medical VA, or social media management.
- No exact visual clone of Amazon interfaces or use of protected Amazon assets.
- No native mobile application in the first release.


## Change control

Anything outside launch scope enters the backlog with a learner problem, expected outcome, cost estimate, test strategy, and explicit tradeoff. Ideas do not enter a sprint merely because they are interesting.
